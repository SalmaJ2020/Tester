About us

Engineering a cleaner climb

We’re chemists, pilots, and energy operators building a scalable pathway to net-zero aviation. Our e-jet uses renewable electricity to split water, combines green hydrogen with captured carbon, and synthesizes drop-in hydrocarbons compatible with today’s jets.

  • Designed as drop-in with existing engines & infrastructure
  • Verified lifecycle accounting & certificates
  • Backed by long-term offtake & airport partnerships

How it works

  1. Renewable power → H₂: Water electrolysis yields green hydrogen.
  2. CO₂ capture: From biogenic or atmospheric sources.
  3. Synthesis: Combine H₂ + CO₂ into e-kerosene; refine to Jet-A spec.
  4. Blend & deliver: Drop-in at your airport via existing fuel logistics.
